Getting started

Open the app, press Start to begin timing, Stop & Log to add a short note, and Reset to clear the current stopwatch session.

  • Use the Todo box to add tasks; check to mark done, Delete to remove.
  • Countdowns show hours and minutes left for nid and uceed.
  • Export creates a clean JPEG summary with totals, entries, todos, and countdowns.

Stopwatch & logging

The stopwatch is wall‑clock based, so it stays accurate even if the tab sleeps; on Stop, enter what you did to log an entry.

  • Space toggles Start/Stop, R resets, E exports.
  • Entries persist locally in your browser.

Export to JPEG

Click Export to generate a shareable summary image with a typographic grid background; it includes totals, entries, todos, and countdowns.

  • Images render client‑side via Canvas; the file downloads as a .jpg.
  • Filenames include timestamp for easy archiving.

Presence badge

A bottom‑right badge displays a simple “studying now” count with day/night ranges for ambient motivation.

  • Day (06:00–22:59): random 1–30; night (23:00–05:59): random 1–15.
  • Refreshes every 3 minutes and on tab focus.

Troubleshooting

  • No export file: ensure the browser allows downloads and try again.
  • Clock drift after sleep: focus the tab; the timer and countdowns refresh immediately.
  • Entries/todos missing: localStorage is per‑browser; clearing site data removes logs.
